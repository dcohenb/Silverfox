01 - About

A Cut
Above

Located in Orinda's iconic Theatre Square, Silver Fox Barber Co. is where traditional barbering meets a modern, refined experience. Since 2022, we've been the neighborhood's go-to for expertly crafted cuts and genuine hospitality.

“Every cut tells a story. We're here to make sure yours looks great.”

Whether you're after a classic scissor cut, a precision fade, or a traditional hot towel shave - our barbers bring skill, care, and attention to every chair.
